Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Mayo
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoe
  • Onion
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Put mayo on one slice of bread
  2. Put mustard on other slice of bread
  3. Add two slices of ham
  4. Add two slices of cheese
  5. Add chopped lettuce
  6. Add sliced tomatos
  7. Add sliced onion
  8. Cut sandwich in half and enjoy
