Healthy Ramen Recipe

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Frozen veggies
  • Water
  • Egg
  • Seasoning pack

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add noodles to water
  3. Add frozen veggies
  4. Simmer for five minutes
  5. Add egg into noodles
  6. Turn off heat
  7. Add seasoning pack
  8. Serve and enjoy
