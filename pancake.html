Oatmeal Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Eggs
  • Ripe banana
  • Vanilla extract
  • Optional: chocolate chips
  • Butter
  • Syrup
  • Optional: mixed berries

Steps

  1. Blend oatmeal, eggs, banana and vanilla in blender
  2. Add butter to hot pan
  3. Add ladle of pancake mix to pan
  4. Wait for small bubbles to apear
  5. Add chocolate chips
  6. Flip pancake
  7. Finish cooking pancake
  8. Repeat until pancake mix is gone
  9. Top with syrup and mixed berrries and serve
