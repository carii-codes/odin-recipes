Yogurt Parfait Recipe

Ingredients

  • Yogurt of choice
  • Straberries
  • Blueberries
  • Granola

Steps

  1. Add a layer of yogurt at bottom of tall glass
  2. Add a layer of blueberries
  3. Add layer of yogurt
  4. Add layer of sliced starberries
  5. Add a layer of yogurt
  6. Add a layer of granola on top
  7. Serve and enjoy
